In the course "User Interface Design2 we made an application for an iPhone. We cooperated with Nidarosdomen, where we found out that they needed an application to inform, appeal to and catch students attention, as well as informing visitors and help them to navigate around.

Offering information to people passing by

Nidarosdomen Cathedral is a well visited tourist attraction, but the locals in the town, most of them students, is one of the hardest customer segments to reach. They seldom plan to visit the cathedral, and the tendency is that ignorance concerning the opnening hours is one of the main issues. People are also often afraid of asking "the wrong people" around in the church, afraid of the possibility of offending the churches men. In order to get information in a discrete and easy way, a smartphone-application is a good solution.
