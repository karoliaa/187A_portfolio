Final Exam, applied modelling in SolidWorks

Assignment text: The aim for this assignment is to test skills and understanding of using 3D-modelling tools in a realistic design process.

The Company "Roros Produkter" want to offer dedicated solutions for garbage collection. They have facilities for building frames in metal (Bending tubes and similar) which they want to utilize. In order to give the product a more sophisticated embodiment they have joined forces with a local boat producer, which opens up for flexible exterior design. Using glassfibre reinforced plastics, and small scale production techniques. To get a flying start for the project, RP has come to you asking for a concept study, of a three wheeled, electrically powered garbage collection vehicle. As you are known for your modelling skills, the company assumes that this can be accomplished within 40 hours of working time.
